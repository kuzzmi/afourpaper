For media. It'll blow your mind.

It's better to see something once than to read about it a thousand times. If you are a publisher you can offer more for your readers. You can extend your newspaper with your clients' smartphones. You can encode and print a .3GP video file which can be recognized by a smartphone.

Something in the background. Checkmate.

We were able to save data to a 19 to 19 cm square image data file, print (150dpi, only back and white) and scan(600dpi) to get the data back. We are talking about 175 kBytes of data. (With no redundancy, as it turns out it was enough for this small printing resolution). We made a whole backend, the algorithms already scale with different printer resolutions (so you can use it with 50 dpi, 150 dpi or even 600 dpi). Our goal was really to provide a stable backend so other developers can simply add a frontend for every device (IOS, Android etc.)!
